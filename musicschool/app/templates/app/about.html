About Pineland Music School


Music School for everyone!


Mika Williams has been teaching music for several years and has recently decided to create Pinelands Music School where there are many students from different backgrounds of instruments and languages.

The instruments that we offer:

  • Electric Guitar
  • Piano
  • Violin
  • Drums
  • Acoustic Guitar
  • Saxophone and many more

The languages we offer are:

  • English
  • Arabic
  • Chinese
  • Spanish
  • Portuguese
  • Hindi and many more

So come on down and be part of the community! It takes a minute to sign up! It doesn't matter what experience you have, all are welcome! (Ages 10-18 require parent permission) For more details on where we are located and our contact information, please visit the contacts page.
